# Captain Gareth's Stonebridge Deployment Plan

- Captain Gareth presented his plan for the potential Stonebridge deployment.
- 50 men-at-arms (2 units of 25), 20 archers.
- Advance party of 10 mounted men-at-arms to secure the river crossing on our side.
- Main body to follow, establish a fortified camp overlooking the bridge.
- Archers to take positions with good sightlines of the Baron's tower and approaches.
- Rotations and resupply lines detailed.
- Plan seems sound and achievable within 48 hours of order. Lord Robert has it under review.
