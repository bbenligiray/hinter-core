# Stonebridge Deployment Underway & Second Letter Sent

- Captain Gareth marched with 50 men-at-arms and 20 archers for Stonebridge at dawn. They should reach our side of the bridge by midday.
- As per Lord Robert's instruction, the second, more strongly worded letter has been dispatched by fast rider to Baron William, timed to arrive after our forces are visible.
- The letter demands cessation of his fortifications and withdrawal of excess troops from Stonebridge, citing it as a provocation.
- The castle is on alert. Remaining garrison is on double watches.
- This is a significant escalation. We pray the Baron chooses de-escalation.
