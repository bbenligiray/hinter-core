# Lady Eleanor Arrives

- Lady Eleanor and her small retinue arrived this afternoon. Honor guard was present, and she was pleased with the reception.
- The welcome banquet is tonight.
- Had a brief preliminary chat with her. She is keen to discuss her niece's betrothal with Lord Robert tomorrow.
- She also inquired discreetly about the Easthaven sickness, having heard whispers on the road. I assured her we are taking all precautions and it is currently contained.
- She seems a sharp and observant woman.
