# Stonebridge: Show of Force Ordered

- After reviewing Captain Gareth's deployment plan and stewing on Baron William's insolent reply for two days, Lord Robert has made his decision.
- He has ordered Captain Gareth to proceed with the show of force at Stonebridge. Deployment to begin at dawn tomorrow.
- The strongly worded letter I drafted is to be dispatched by fast rider as soon as our forces are in position on our side of the bridge.
- Lord Robert's mood is grim. He hopes this show of strength will make the Baron see reason without bloodshed, but is prepared for the alternative.
- All other castle activities are now secondary to this deployment and the Easthaven quarantine.
