# Betrothal Discussion: Lord Robert & Lady Eleanor

- Lord Robert and Lady Eleanor met this morning to discuss the betrothal of her niece, Catherine, to Lord Robert's ward, young William.
- The discussion was amicable. Lady Eleanor is agreeable to the match in principle.
- Dowry terms were discussed broadly. Lady Eleanor proposes lands near the Silverstream, which are fertile. Lord Robert seemed receptive.
- Further details on the marriage contract and timeline to be worked out by their scribes/lawyers. Lady Eleanor will send her representative next month.
- Overall, a positive step. This alliance would be beneficial.
