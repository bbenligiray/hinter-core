# Messenger to Baron William Dispatched

- Young Peter, carrying Lord Robert's diplomatic message regarding Stonebridge, departed for Baron William's lands at first light.
- He carries a white flag and is instructed to deliver the message directly to the Baron or his seneschal, and await a formal reply if offered, but not to tarry unduly.
- Peter is a good lad, level-headed. He understands the delicacy of the mission.
- We now await the Baron's response. This could go several ways.
