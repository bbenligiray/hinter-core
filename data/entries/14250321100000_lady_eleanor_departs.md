# Lady Eleanor Departs

- Lady Eleanor and her retinue departed this morning. Her visit was pleasant and productive.
- Lord Robert is pleased with the outcome of the betrothal discussions. A formal alliance with her house will strengthen our position in the region.
- She expressed her thanks for the hospitality and her sympathies regarding the Easthaven situation, commending Lord Robert's decisive action.
- I have tasked a scribe with preparing a draft of our side of the marriage contract terms based on the discussions.
