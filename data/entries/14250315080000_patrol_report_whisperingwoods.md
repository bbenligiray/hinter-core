# Whispering Woods Patrol Report (Captain Gareth)

- Captain Gareth returned with the patrol from Whispering Woods.
- Encountered a small band of 5-6 bandits. After a brief skirmish, bandits fled. Two captured, three fled.
- Our losses: One man with a minor arrow wound to the arm (treated by Friar Benedictus's man with the patrol), one horse lamed.
- Captured bandits claim to be part of a larger group led by "One-Eyed Jack," but know little of his main camp. They were merely outcasts scavenging.
- Gareth reports the woods are quieter now. He left a small watch at the old shrine for a few days.
- No sign of Baron William's men or any connection to them observed by Gareth.
