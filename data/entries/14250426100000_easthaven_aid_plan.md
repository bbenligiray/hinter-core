# Easthaven Aid Distribution Plan

- Met with Reeve John and Friar Benedictus to discuss the aid package for Easthaven based on the Reeve's report.
- Plan:
    - Food (grain, salted fish, cheese) to be distributed proportionally to household size by the village elder, overseen by Reeve John for fairness.
    - Seed grain (barley and oats) to be provided to all farming families. Prioritize those who lost all stock.
    - Three milk cows and two oxen from the castle's herds will be gifted to the village to be shared or assigned to the neediest families by the elder and Friar.
    - A work party of 4 castle carpenters and 2 laborers will travel to Easthaven next week for 5 days to assist with urgent home repairs. They will camp separately and take their own provisions.
- Lord Robert has approved this plan and the expenditure.
