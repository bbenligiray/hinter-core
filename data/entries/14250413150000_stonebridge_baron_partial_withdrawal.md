# Stonebridge: Baron William Partial Withdrawal

- Captain Gareth reports from Stonebridge: after a week of tense standoff, Baron William's forces have visibly reduced their numbers at the tower.
- They have not fully withdrawn, but the aggressive reinforcement has ceased, and some of the extra men have departed.
- It seems our show of force and the strongly worded letter had some effect, though the Baron would never admit it.
- Lord Robert is cautiously optimistic. He has ordered Captain Gareth to maintain our current posture for now but to prepare for a gradual drawdown if the Baron's side remains quiet.
- No direct reply from the Baron to our second letter, but actions speak louder.
