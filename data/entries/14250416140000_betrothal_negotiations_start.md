# Betrothal Contract Negotiations Commence

- First negotiation session with Master Alaric of Silverstream regarding the marriage contract between young William and Lady Catherine. Maester Giles and I represented Lord Robert.
- Master Alaric is a sharp negotiator. He presented Lady Eleanor's terms, which are largely agreeable but seek a larger portion of the Stonebridge timber rights than Lord Robert initially envisioned.
- We countered with our terms, emphasizing the value of the Silverstream lands offered as dowry.
- Discussions are civil but detailed. Several points on inheritance and wardship of any children will require careful wording.
- Further sessions scheduled for the next two days.
