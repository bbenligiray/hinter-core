# Stonebridge Deployment - Gradual Drawdown

- After more than a week with no further provocations from Baron William at Stonebridge, and his forces remaining at their reduced level, Lord Robert has authorized a gradual drawdown of our own contingent.
- Captain Gareth will pull back half the men-at-arms and archers over the next two days.
- A strong watch will remain in place, and the camp will be maintained for rapid reoccupation if needed.
- This will ease the strain on our garrison and allow men to return to other duties.
- Still no formal reply from the Baron to the second letter, but his actions (or lack thereof) are telling.
