# Blacksmith & Fletcher Dispute Resolved

- Followed up with Blacksmith John and Fletcher Thomas.
- After much grumbling, they agreed to the compromise: Blacksmith offers a 5% discount on a bulk order of good quality steel rods for arrowheads, Fletcher guarantees to use these exclusively for castle arrows and cease sourcing inferior heads.
- Both shook hands, albeit reluctantly. Hopefully, this improves arrow quality and supply.
- Informed Captain Gareth. He can now proceed with the Oakhaven fletcher contact with a clearer picture of our internal arrowhead situation. The draft report to him can be revised and sent.
