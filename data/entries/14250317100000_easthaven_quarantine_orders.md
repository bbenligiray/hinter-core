# Easthaven Quarantine & Orders for Sergeant Miles

- Discussed Sergeant Miles' report from Easthaven with Lord Robert.
- Lord Robert orders a strict quarantine of Easthaven. No one in or out without his express permission. This is to prevent spread to other villages and the castle itself.
- Rules of Engagement for Sgt Miles: Use minimal force to maintain the quarantine. Healthy villagers attempting to flee are to be turned back, with force if necessary but avoid bloodshed. If they become violent, defend yourselves. Infected individuals are not to be approached closely.
- Rations for Miles' detachment will be sent tomorrow. They are to remain until the situation stabilizes or further orders.
- A messenger has been dispatched to Sgt Miles with these orders.
