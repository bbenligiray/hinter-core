# Sergeant Miles - Third Report from Easthaven

- Urgent message from Sergeant Miles. The sickness in Easthaven is spreading faster. Five more deaths.
- The provisions cart was received, but fear is making distribution difficult. Some families refuse to leave their homes even for food.
- Miles reports his own men are getting anxious, though none are sick yet. They are running low on fresh water as the village well is now considered contaminated by many.
- He strongly requests Friar Benedictus himself to come, if his health allows, as his presence might calm the populace and his knowledge is desperately needed.
- This is a dire turn. Must discuss with Lord Robert and the Friar immediately.
