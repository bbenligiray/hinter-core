# Willow Creek Grain Shipment Arrived

- The grain shipment from Willow Creek, escorted by Merchant Thomas's men, has finally arrived. Two days later than the revised ETA of 14th, but intact.
- Quality appears good. Instructed quartermaster to oversee its proper storage in the newly aired silo.
- This brings our stores to nearly 90% capacity. A good buffer.
- Merchant Thomas sends his apologies for the further delay, citing a broken wagon axle.
