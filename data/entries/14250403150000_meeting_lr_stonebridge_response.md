# Meeting with Lord Robert: Response to Baron William

- Attended Lord Robert in his solar as summoned. Captain Gareth was also present.
- Lord Robert is indeed furious at the Baron's dismissive reply regarding Stonebridge. He considers it a deliberate slight.
- We reviewed our current troop strength, readiness of the garrison, and supplies.
- Options discussed:
    1.  A stronger diplomatic protest, perhaps via a herald.
    2.  A show of force: marching a significant portion of our garrison to our side of the Stonebridge.
    3.  Attempting to forcibly dismantle the Baron's new fortifications (highest risk).
- Lord Robert is leaning towards a show of force (Option 2) to signal he will not be trifled with, but wants to avoid open conflict if possible, especially with Easthaven still a concern.
- He has tasked Captain Gareth with preparing a plan for deploying 50 men-at-arms and 20 archers to the Stonebridge border within 48 hours if ordered.
- I am to draft a very strongly worded letter to Baron William, to be sent IF the show of force is enacted, demanding he cease fortifications and remove excess troops.
