# A Quiet Week - Summer Preparations

- The past week has been blessedly uneventful. Stonebridge remains quiet, Easthaven aid is flowing.
- Took the opportunity to review summer work schedules:
    - Road repairs on the King's Highway section.
    - Clearing the lower pastures for summer grazing.
    - Reinforcing the mill dam before summer storms.
- Also began drafting plans for the harvest festival. It's important to keep morale high after the recent troubles.
- The alliance with Lady Eleanor's house feels secure. Young William seems to be warming to the idea of marriage.
- Awaiting first delivery from Oakhaven fletcher next month.
