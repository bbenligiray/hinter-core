# Marriage Contract Signed - Alliance with House of Eleanor Formalized

- Master Alaric of Silverstream departed today, carrying a copy of the marriage contract for young William and Lady Catherine, signed and sealed by Lord Robert.
- Lady Eleanor's seal was already upon the copy he brought, so the agreement is now fully ratified.
- The terms are favorable, securing a strong alliance and valuable dowry lands, with acceptable concessions on timber rights.
- This is a major diplomatic success for Lord Robert and a cause for quiet celebration. The wedding is tentatively planned for late summer.
