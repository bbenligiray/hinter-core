# Sergeant Miles - Second Report from Easthaven

- Messenger from Miles. Quarantine is holding. Villagers are unhappy but compliant for now.
- Two more deaths overnight. Friar Benedictus's man is doing what he can for the sick, but lacks supplies for all.
- Miles reports some of the healthier villagers are asking for permission to hunt in nearby woods for food, as their own stores are dwindling and they fear using communal stores touched by the sick.
- He awaits orders on this request. Also reiterates need for ongoing ration supply for his men.
