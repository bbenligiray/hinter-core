# Herbs from Oakhaven Received

- Young Thomas returned from Oakhaven. Good lad, made quick time.
- Successfully procured a good quantity of feverfew and comfrey. Also brought back some yarrow, as the apothecary there suggested it for lung fevers.
- Friar Benedictus was very grateful. The herbs were dispatched to him immediately.
- Thomas reported Oakhaven itself is well, no signs of the Easthaven sickness there. He avoided Easthaven as instructed.
