# Report for Lady Eleanor - Regarding: Your Upcoming Visit

## Information / Opportunity / Insight
My Lady, Lord Robert personally wishes to express his keen anticipation of your visit, and we all eagerly await your arrival. The guest chambers have been prepared to your liking, and the kitchens are making special preparations for a welcome banquet. An honor guard will be ready to receive you.

Lord Robert also asked me to convey his readiness to discuss the betrothal arrangements for your niece at your convenience during your stay.

## Context / Background (If necessary for this item)
All preparations are proceeding smoothly.

## Suggested Action / Next Steps (If applicable to this item)
Please inform us of your expected arrival day and time, if known, so we may make final arrangements for your reception.
