# Mason's Assessment of North Wall Cracks

- Met with John Mason. He confirms the new cracks near the old tower are concerning but not immediately critical.
- Attributes them to settling and recent freeze-thaw cycles.
- Recommends repointing with fresh mortar before the heavy spring rains. Estimates 3 days work for two men.
- Will provide a written summary for Lord Robert's preliminary report.
- Cost for materials will be minimal, mostly labor.
