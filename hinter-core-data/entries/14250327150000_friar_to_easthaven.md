# Friar Benedictus Agrees to Go to Easthaven

- Spoke with Friar Benedictus regarding Sergeant Miles' urgent request.
- The good Friar, despite his age and the risks, has agreed to travel to Easthaven. He says it is his duty.
- Lord Robert has approved, on condition the Friar takes every precaution. He will travel with a small escort (2 guards, separate from Miles' men), take his own water and provisions, and avoid direct contact with the sickest where possible, guiding others in their care.
- He plans to depart tomorrow at dawn. This is a brave act. May God protect him.
- Sent word to Sergeant Miles that the Friar is coming.
