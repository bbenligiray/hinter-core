# Captain Gareth - Oakhaven Fletcher Contact

- Captain Gareth reports he sent a rider to the Oakhaven fletcher.
- The fletcher, Master Alaric, has a good stock of seasoned yew staves and can supply 200 longbows within two months, and 500 arrows per month thereafter.
- His prices are reasonable, slightly higher than our local fletcher but quality is reputed to be excellent.
- Gareth recommends placing an initial order for 100 bows and 1000 arrows.
- He also confirmed Alaric can supply the herbs Friar Benedictus needed (though we already sourced them via young Thomas, good to know for future).
