# Betrothal Contract Agreed

- After three days of intense but respectful negotiation with Master Alaric, we have reached an agreement on all terms for the marriage contract between young William and Lady Catherine.
- The Stonebridge timber rights were the main sticking point. We conceded a slightly larger share than initially planned, but secured stronger clauses regarding mutual defense and trade considerations in return. Maester Giles was invaluable here.
- Master Alaric will depart tomorrow with the agreed terms for Lady Eleanor's final seal. Lord Robert has already given his.
- This is a significant achievement. The alliance will be a cornerstone for years to come.
