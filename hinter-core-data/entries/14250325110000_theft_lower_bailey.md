# Theft in Lower Bailey

- Guards reported a minor theft from the cooper's workshop - some tools and a small cask of ale.
- Investigation was swift. Perpetrator was a new scullery boy, recently arrived from a village near Easthaven (though he claims to have left before the sickness).
- Boy confessed, said he was desperate. Tools recovered.
- Lord Robert orders 10 lashes and dismissal from castle service. He is to be escorted beyond our lands.
- A reminder to be vigilant about newcomers, especially with current troubles.
