# Messenger from Baron William

- A messenger arrived from Baron William. Cordial greetings, claims the Baron will be hunting near our borders for a few days.
- This aligns with Friar Benedictus' report of his riders near the old mill.
- Lord Robert is suspicious. Instructed me to double the watch on the western approaches and report any large armed parties.
- No mention of the ongoing border dispute over the Stonebridge.
