# Reeve John's Report on Easthaven Needs

- Reeve John returned from Easthaven. His assessment is sobering.
- Food stores are critically low, the castle provisions were a stopgap. Seed for spring planting is almost entirely gone.
- At least 7 families have lost their primary worker (father/husband). 12 children orphaned or semi-orphaned.
- Some homes are falling into disrepair from neglect. Several families lost their only milk cow or ox.
- The village elder is overwhelmed but grateful for our attention.
- We need to organize a significant aid package: more food, seed grain, perhaps some livestock from castle herds, and a work party to help with essential repairs before summer.
