# Scout Report: Stonebridge Activity

- One of the western scouts, Tom, reported back.
- He noted an unusual increase in armed men near the Stonebridge, on Baron William's side of the border. Not a large force, but more than usual patrols.
- They appear to be reinforcing the small watchtower the Baron built there last year, the one that caused the initial dispute.
- No overt aggression, but their presence is a clear statement.
- This comes as Baron William's "hunting trip" should be concluding.
- Lord Robert informed. He is displeased but not surprised. Instructed continued discreet observation.
