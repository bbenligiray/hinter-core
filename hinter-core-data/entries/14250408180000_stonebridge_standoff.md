# Stonebridge Standoff

- Captain Gareth reports from Stonebridge. Our forces are in position on our side of the river, camp established.
- Baron William's men have been seen observing our deployment from their reinforced tower and along their side of the bank. They are numerous but have made no hostile moves.
- The second letter from Lord Robert should have reached the Baron by now.
- The atmosphere is extremely tense. Gareth's men are on high alert.
- Lord Robert is awaiting any response from the Baron, or any change in his troop posture.
