# Friar Benedictus Returns - Easthaven Quarantine to Lift

- Friar Benedictus returned from Easthaven today, looking weary but well. Sergeant Miles and his men also returned, all healthy.
- The Friar reports the sickness has passed. No new cases in five days. He has advised the villagers on cleaning and purifying their homes and water sources.
- Lord Robert has commended the Friar and Miles for their service.
- Based on this, Lord Robert has ordered the Easthaven quarantine to be lifted in stages:
    - Day 1-3: Limited supervised access for trade of essential goods only.
    - Day 4-7: Free movement for Easthaven villagers to their fields, but not to other villages.
    - Day 8 onwards: Full lifting if no resurgence.
- The "shadow" talk among villagers seems to have faded with their recovery, the Friar attributes it to fever dreams.
