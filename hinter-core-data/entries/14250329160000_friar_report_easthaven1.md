# Friar Benedictus - First Report from Easthaven

- A rider returned with Friar Benedictus's first report from Easthaven.
- He confirms the sickness is severe and widespread. He has begun tending to the afflicted, and his presence seems to have calmed some of the immediate panic.
- The herbs from Oakhaven are a great boon, but he fears they will not be enough if this continues.
- He is working with Sergeant Miles and the village elder to organize care and ensure the dead are buried with proper rites, away from the village well.
- He notes the villagers are deeply frightened of starvation due to the quarantine, despite the provisions sent.
- He sends his blessings and prayers.
