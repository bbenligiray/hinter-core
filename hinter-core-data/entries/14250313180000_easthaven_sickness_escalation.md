# Easthaven Sickness - Escalation & Action

- Received Friar Benedictus's grim update on the lung fever in Easthaven: a dozen households, three dead. This is serious.
- Lord Robert briefed. He is deeply concerned.
- Decision:
    1. Expedite herb acquisition. Tasked young Thomas (stablehand, reliable) to ride to Oakhaven at first light with funds for feverfew & comfrey. He is to bypass Easthaven.
    2. Send a small detachment (5 men under Sergeant Miles) to Easthaven to assess the situation, offer support to the village elder, and maintain order if panic sets in. They are to avoid direct contact with the sick if possible and report back daily.
- Need to monitor if this affects any trade routes or if other villages report similar outbreaks.
