# Easthaven Hunting Request - Decision

- Presented Sergeant Miles' report and the villagers' hunting request to Lord Robert.
- After consideration, Lord Robert has DENIED the request for villagers to hunt outside Easthaven.
    - Rationale: Too high risk of individuals breaking quarantine and potentially spreading sickness, even if they claim to be healthy. The integrity of the quarantine is paramount.
- Instead, Lord Robert has ordered a cart of basic provisions (flour, salted meat, dried peas) to be sent from castle stores to Easthaven, to be left at a safe distance for the village elder to distribute. This should alleviate immediate food concerns.
- Messenger dispatched to Sergeant Miles with this decision and news of the provision cart.
