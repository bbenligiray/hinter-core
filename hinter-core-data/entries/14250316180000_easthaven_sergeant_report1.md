# Sergeant Miles - First Report from Easthaven

- Messenger from Sergeant Miles arrived.
- He confirms the Friar's assessment: sickness is widespread, morale low. Village elder is struggling to maintain calm.
- His men are helping reinforce a makeshift barrier at the village edge, as some villagers are trying to flee to other settlements, risking spread.
- No signs of banditry yet, but the situation is tense.
- He requests clarification on rules of engagement if uninfected villagers try to force their way out.
- Also requests more rations if his detachment is to stay longer than 2-3 days.
